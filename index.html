About Jujifina

Jujifina is not just a vocal producer and recording artist - she is also a music executive of LQD ENT and the founder of Official Afro Vybz, also known as AfroVybz(OAV). This cultural movement, led by Jujifina, showcases her musical expertise and artists through music, culture, and dance, expanding their brand and reaching others through the diaspora connection. More than just entertainment, Official Afro Vybz is about inclusion, education, and love.

As a polyglot, linguist, and culture enthusiast, Jujifina uses her diverse skill set to connect with a wide range of people that the average monolingual cannot. She has collaborated with artists such as Flama de la Section, Eddy Ranks, Temptation (Tentacion), Mark6ix, and Rocksteady, who was formerly signed to Nigerian icon 2Face.

Jujifina works closely with artists worldwide as a writer, vocal producer, brander, marketer, social media community builder, and influencer. She manages artists like Dilo Swagz, Mark6ix Okpani, Cigity, Winna Boy, and Rocksteady, while collaborating with many others in the industry. Get ready to be inspired and connected through the power of music and culture with Jujifina at the helm.
